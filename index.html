About Me

"That 13 year old who programs"

I am self taught, 13 year old programmer, that specializes in web design and game development. I program in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Lua, C#, C++, etc. I started programming in 2020 with Lua in Roblox Studio. I developed my interest for programming by taking online courses on Udemy and learning with online Zoom Classes. I have learned basically all my web design skills from these courses and also learned languages like C# with Unity, and Python.

I started learning web development by making websites with my friends about a fictional company, which grew my love to web design in general. Later after that, I get a request from my Uncle to build a website for him and it was my first time building a truly professional website. The website is called Shulman Realtor. I also got paid $100 for the building the website, and $20 each month to maintain the website.
