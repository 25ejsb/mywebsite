About Me

Adam Shulman

A One Man Team

Adam Shulman has been passionately selling residential real estate in the Boston area for over 5 years. Upon transitioning from his first career in social work, Adam found his true calling in real estate. He utilizes a client-focused approach, enthusiastically relishing every opportunity to help buyers, sellers, and renters alike. Adam joined RE/MAX Destiny in 2021 and reached the 100% Club in just 7 months.
 

Adam appreciates that this process is more than just a transaction it is a series of important and often emotional decisions. As such, he strives to be respectful of individual differences and preferences. Adam delivers consistently positive results by clearly and efficiently communicating with agents, lawyers, lenders, landlords, and anyone else who may be involved in a client’s next chapter of life.
 

Adam grew up in Newton and went to school at Bates College in Maine. In his spare time, he enjoys live music, comedy shows, skiing, and playing with his beloved cat Oliver.
 

A portion of every commission is given to the Children’s Miracle Network. For all your residential real estate needs, you can count on Adam to successfully be of service!
